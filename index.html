Hello! I am Mahaveer, a Ph.D. scholar at the Indian Institute of Technology Mandi.

I specialize in High Harmonic Generation (HHG) and Attosecond Science, which are cutting-edge areas of research in ultrafast optics and laser physics.

My research focuses on understanding the generation of high-energy photons and ultrashort light pulses on the attosecond timescale, with applications in various fields including spectroscopy, imaging, and quantum information processing.
